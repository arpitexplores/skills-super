## Why SUPER Skills For AI Vibe Coding

Super Marketing Execution helps AI agents move from idea to shipped campaign. Use it for launch plans, landing page CRO, conversion copy, social content systems, email sequences, paid ad structure, tracking plans, and post-campaign learning loops.

These skills are designed for **AI vibe coding**: fast, agent-assisted building where the AI needs strong domain context, practical workflows, guardrails, and implementation-ready outputs.

**SEO and discovery keywords:** marketing execution, campaign orchestration, CRO, conversion copywriting, landing page optimisation, email marketing, social content, paid ads, UTM tracking, growth marketing, AI vibe coding.

## Install

Copy this folder into your agent's skills directory, then restart or reload the agent.

```bash
cp -R super-marketing-execution ~/.your-agent/skills/
```

Use it by name:

```text
Use $super-marketing-execution to help with this request.
```
